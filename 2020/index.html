Welcome!

Data I/O is a great event to get concentrated practice with data and analytical tools while collaborating with peers, professors, and professionals. No analytics experience needed! Groups of 1-4 will work on projects relating to AI and predictive algorithms, data visualization, and/or actionable insights. Open source data sets will be provided, but groups may also bring their own if preferred. Judges will award prizes for best overall project, runner-up, best visualization, and best insights. Mentors will be available to help and network with throughout the day.
